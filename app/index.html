DITSS Surveillance Cameras Coverage Map - Ekkersrijt

Demo client for the Cameras 2D FoV service. This prototype app is a coverage map of surveilled areas. The cameras are the DITSS surveillance cameras and the area of study is the Ekkersrijt industrial area, located north of Eindhoven (NL).
